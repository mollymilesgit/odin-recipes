Cookie Dough

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Granulated Sugar
  • Brown Sugar
  • Vanilla Extract
  • Chocolate Chips

Steps

  1. Cook flour in oven for 10 minutes to proof and kill bacteria
  2. Microwave butter until melted
  3. Mix both sugar and butter
  4. Incorporate liquid mix and solids
  5. Add vanilla extract and chocolate chips
  6. Chill in fridge for 15 minutes and serve
