Orange Chicken

Ingredients

  • Boneless Chicken Thighs
  • Orange Juice
  • Granulated Sugar
  • Soy Sauce
  • Chili Powder
  • Red Chili Flakes
  • Cornstarch
  • 2 Room Temp Eggs

Steps

  1. Simmer orange juice with all ingredients mixed in minus the chicken and Eggs until thicken stir often
  2. Batter chicken with egg and cornstarch in that order
  3. Fry until chicken is at safe temp
  4. Let sit for 5 minutes and then fry again for a few minutes
  5. Coat chicken in sauce and then make rice in rice cooker, serve when ready
