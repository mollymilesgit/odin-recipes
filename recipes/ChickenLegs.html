State Fair Chicken Legs

This is a recipe I modified to make state fair turkey legs that can also be used for chicken!

Ingredients

  • Paprika
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Chili Powder
  • Garlic Powder
  • Chicken or Turkey Legs
  • Cooking Oil

Steps

  1. Brine Chicken in Salt Water Overnight
  2. Drain and Pat Legs Dry
  3. Make rub with Paprika, Salt, Pepper, Chili, and Garlic Powder then mix in a spoonful of cooking oil
  4. Preheat oven to 425
  5. Coat Chicken or Turkey with rub evenly as possible
  6. Roast for 45 minutes or until the legs are to proper cooking temp
  7. Let cool for about 10-15 minutes
